Before you start

Road races, parades, bike races, charity walks, and similar events that use public roads, sidewalks or greenways will require a Special Event Permit. Please refer to our Special Event Planning Guide to learn about the policies and procedures involved in hosting a road race event in Raleigh, including application and permit fees, application deadlines, specific limitations on race locations and overall race caps. If applicable, you can view the Greenway Section Map.

Your proposed event will not be reviewed if you submit your application after the 120-day deadline or if you do not pay the corresponding $100 non-refundable application fee. Submitting an application does NOT guarantee that your event will be approved.

How to Apply (all steps must be completed prior to 120-day deadline):

  1. Due to the restrictions and other policies on Road Races and Parades, we highly recommend that event organizers contact the Special Events Office prior to applying to confirm date/location availability
  2. Complete the Road Race and Parade Application
  3. Submit the $100 non-refundable application fee over the phone, in person, or via mail. Instructions for payment submittal are included on the Application Fee Payment Form. Please complete this form and submit it with your mail or in-person payment. If you choose to pay over the phone, please be prepared to give this information verbally.
